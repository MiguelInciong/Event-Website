Why Footprints for Fairness

The path to progress is only possible to seen when we are all suporting one another. The world has had enough of unfairness and injustices it is time for change, it is time for progress.

Organized By

A group of adamson university information technology students led by Karl Inciong who were willing to instigate change and raise awareness on the social inequality not just students but people all over the world are experiencing.
